Projects

Zoo Seeker

An Android Application made in CSE 110 that provides a guided tour of our very own San Diego Zoo

A responsive app using Android Studio with search and planning features for exhibits at the San Diego Zoo. The app has GPS utility included and can use the device's GPS feature to give real time location tracking to exhibits at the zoo. The zoo's data can be updated and changed at any time depending on the dataset fed to the application. This project was a collaboartion between 6 people over an 8 week long school project that followed an Agile Software Development style.
